LAND & PLANNING

From our initial site meeting we’ll work through the council planning requirements, resource consent issues if applicable, the geo-tech reporting and all site considerations pertaining to the build of your project. Our team will ensure expert advice is at hand throughout each step of the planning process.

We take care of the council process - from initial reviews through to execution of building consents and amendments.

Our architectural team will be matched to you and your project to ensure the right fit. Part of our bespoke service is ensuring the right designer is on board throughout the design process.

INTERIOR DESIGN

With a passion for crafted interiors our interiors team will help guide you through kitchens, bathrooms, lighting and lighting plans, flooring and finishes such as window treatments. We’ll ensure you have the latest design options available to the market at your fingertips and advise on tried and tested materials.

From detailing your lighting plan, home automation, environmental and sustainable attributes, and security our team of experts can walk you through the options that suit your lifestyle and your budget.
